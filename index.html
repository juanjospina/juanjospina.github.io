Description

The Home Energy Managament System project consists of the design and implementenation of a central energy controller in charge of automating the power consumption of a business or home client by controlling the loads according to real-time price-based information.

STUDIES - ACADEMIC INFORMATION

The Goals of the Project :

  • Automation based on real-time pricing.
  • Control of loads based on user input.
  • Communication via 2.4 Ghz RF/Wireless technology.
  • Energy efficient and low power consumptiom.
  • "Smart" algorithm discerning critical from non-critical loads.
  • Real-Time Web interface control.

The system is designed to be use in both residential and commercial settings, in which real time pricing is actively provided by the utility company. The system is also designed for a customer that desires to control loads over a network.

PUBLICATIONS OR PROJECTS...
