Summary

I am a researcher and engineer currently working as a Scientist at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL). I received a dual B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering, a M.S. in Electrical Engineering, and a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Florida State University in 2016, 2018, and 2019 respectively.

My professional experience is focused on the research and development of intelligent control systems for power systems and smart-grid applications, renewable energy integration, cybersecurity, and real-time simulation. I believe in a sustainable, secure, and energy-efficient society driven by innovation and automation; that is why I find rewarding working in areas related to machine learning, data analysis, reinforcement learning, optimization, real-time simulation, and cyber-security with applications related to the power and energy fields.
